Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 18, 2025

Overview

Frontier Minilab ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data We Collect

Frontier Minilab does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All photo editing happens locally on your device.

Photo Library Access

The app requests access to your photo library to:

  • Load photos for editing
  • Save edited photos back to your library

Your photos are never uploaded to any server. All processing happens on-device.

Google Sign-In (Optional)

If you choose to use Google Drive export, you may sign in with your Google account. This connection is used solely to:

  • Authenticate with Google Drive
  • Upload photos you choose to export

We do not access any other Google account data.

Analytics

Frontier Minilab does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

Data Storage

App preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integration is Google Drive (optional). Google's privacy policy applies to their services: policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

Frontier Minilab does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at:
support@frontierminilab.com
